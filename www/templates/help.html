How to use

To use this app, choose either a game or movie to search for. Then, enter the title of the game/movie you want to view. If more than one game/movie matches the title, you will be required to select which game/movie you want to see details for, from the list displayed.

The game/movie's aggregated review score, along with additional information will then be displayed.

If you see a game/movie you wish to save, for quick viewing of the score later, simply press the 'Favourite' button at the bottom of the details page.

No locations being displayed

When on the game details page, if you do not see any locations appearing, follow these steps:

  • 1: Enable location services on your device.
  • 2: If needed, allow this app to access your location details.
  • 3: Restart the app (Close fully, and re-open).
